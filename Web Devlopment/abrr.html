ABOUT US


Helping VIT-AP Students to have a better Hostel Experience with our catering service


Our food courts in VIT-AP serve hygienic multi-cuisine food to the students. We offer a wide range of cuisine like Indian, Continental, Western and regional cuisine like Andhra, Tamil Nadu and North Indian and authentic Chinese cuisine apart from our confectionaries and desserts.


What sets us apart is the variety of foods we serve for our clients.
