ABOUT US

Helping VIT-AP Students to have a better Hostel Experience with our catering service

CRCL LLP is the largest domestically managed contractual food services company in India serving over 2 lakhs multi-ethnic meals per day. It caters to corporates, industrial units, educational institutions, and healthcare establishments.
CRCL’s incredible journey started in VITAP and since then its commitment to quality, customer satisfaction and passion for serving people have driven the company towards success and secure its place in the hearts of people.
